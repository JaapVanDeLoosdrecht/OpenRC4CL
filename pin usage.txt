- Timer: use TH for button and SWRL1/2 for External led on Timer
- BAT- connecyted to GND
  IAO ESP32C6, GND (in the 7*2 pins) and BAT- (on the backside) is actually connected together.
  Safe to tie grounds together, you can safely reference BAT- to any GND point on your PCB.
  But you cannot bypass the BAT pads for charging. If you want to use the onboard charger, 
  you must route the battery through the bottom BAT pads.

Phases in connecting:
 Tx || - | H | T | + ||
	||-------------------------
HT1 ||   |   |   | - || H  T  +
HT2 ||	 |   | - | H || T  +
HT3 ||   | - | H | T || +
HT4 || - | H | T | - ||

HT1: Tx T floating=0, Tx H floating=+ -> Hold on

HT2: Tx T floating=0 or +, Tx H floating=+ -> Hold on
