- Analog/Radio Frequency (RF): Do not place ground fill directly under antennas (like on an ESP32 or radio module).
- Antenna
  You may need to reduce the output power. For some reason, the supermini has issues running at max power, 
  which I believe is the default setting. There are a few potential issues with this device that can often 
  be resolved through configuration, including your specific problem. I suggest taking a look at a great video 
  that I found: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IzLD6f8cDHs&list=WL&index=3 which offers several tips for 
  using this device. I don't, however, recommend reducing the power to its minimum level, as suggested in the video. 
  Try some of the mid-way power level settings, perhaps WIFI_POWER_11dBm, or WIFI_POWER_7dBm. 
  I suspect a lot of the product reviews that report faulty modules might have been resolvable by following these tips. 
  The chip antennas do work, but they are more directional than the PCB antennas; so, attention to the orientation of the device is recommend.
